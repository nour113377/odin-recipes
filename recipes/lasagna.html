Lasagna

A glorious finished lasagna

We lasagna by layering pasta, bolognese sauce, and mascarpone, and we bake it.

Look at the ingredients below and work your magic.

  • pasta
  • bolognese
  • mascarpone
  1. layer bolognese in your tray
  2. add pasta, mascarpone, then pasta again
  3. continue step 1 and 2 until its the desired height
  4. grate cheese on top, and bake until golden
